  <h2>About the Tsogo Alumni Society</h2>
  <p>The Tsogo Alumni Society was founded to bring together alumni of <strong>Tsogo High School</strong> (later Tsogo Secondary School) in Pretoria, South Africa. From 2009 to 2020 we organised career expos, bursary programmes, networking evenings, school infrastructure projects, and community initiatives — all run by volunteers.</p>
  <p>We are registered as a Non-Profit Organisation: <strong>053-952-NPO</strong>.</p>

  <hr>

  <h2>Current Status</h2>
  <p>The Society has been <strong>on hiatus since 2020</strong>. There is no active executive committee at this time. This website is preserved as an archive of our work and community history.</p>
  <p>We hope the archive continues to inspire current learners and remind alumni of what we built together.</p>
  <p>If you are a Tsogo alumnus who would like to help revive the Society, we would love to hear from you.</p>
