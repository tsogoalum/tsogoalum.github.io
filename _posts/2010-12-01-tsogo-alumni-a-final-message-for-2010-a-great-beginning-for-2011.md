Dear Fellow Alumni and Current Tsogo Learners

Allow us to wish you the best as you wrap up 2010. We also wish the Matrics the best of luck with their results. We hope their hard work has paid off. For all of those who have graduated in 2010 and have now moved on to the next stage in their lives we also wish them luck in their coming adventures. To all Alumni we wish that 2010 was what you expected it to be but we compel you to look forward to 2011 as a year when you yourself will surpass your own expectations.

As the Tsogo Directory Team, let us be first to say that this has been a great year. We grew our reach to Alumni as well as collaborated well with the [Alumni Society](/2010/society/). We wish to encourage all alumni to look for a space where they can fit in within the Alumni Society. The current executive committee of the Alumni Society is made up of:

- **Chairperson**:  Ms. Motlalepula Phakedi
- **Deputy Chairperson**: Mr. Pono Mogoera
- **Treasurer**:  Mr. Malebo Chadi
- **Secretary**:  [Mr. Thabo Ncalo](/2010/2009/08/alumni-profile-thabo-ncalo)
- **Events Co-coordinators**:  Mr. Botshelo Letswalo &  Mr. Kabelo Modikwe
- **Project Co-ordinators**:  Mr. Moses Mokgoko  &  Mr. Prince Kotu
- **Additional Exec Member**:  Ms. Thabiso Mokwena

Their universal email is [tsogoalumni.exco@gmail.com](mailto:tsogoalumni.exco@gmail.com), For the latest news on the Alumni Society please look out for emails as well as visit their web page [/society/](/2010/society/). For 2010 the Society hosted a price giving for Leaners and Teachers earlier in the year, The Annual Career fair at Tsogo and 2 Alumni Homecoming weekends. If you could not make it for the events we encourage you to make 2011 a year in which you are more involved. A number of alumni have started personal projects with the school through the society or have looked for ways in which they can assist. Again visit [/society/](/2010/society/) for more information.

Our alumni are diverse and we wish for them to serve as inspiration as well as possible mentors to others. We want to encourage networking between alumni and will be looking for more ways to connect alumni. If you want to nominate anyone(including yourself) please email us at [tsogoalum@gmail.com](mailto:tsogoalum@gmail.com). We are now also accepting entries of Alumni in the News. So if you are an alum and there is a news story about you somewhere we encourage that you submit that to us so that we can show case the talent that we have. We have also been accepting written pieces (essays etc.) in 2010 and will now actively encourage them. Two such pieces are:

- [Life is a Journey Not A Destination](/2010/2010/09/life-is-a-journey-not-a-destination) – Keabetswe Mogase
- [Commemorating Women’s Month](/2010/2010/08/commemorating-womens-month) –  Botlhale Mabatshidi Nong

If you would like to assist in writing we welcome any input. You can send us any memorabilia like pictures or stories that you have about your experiences at and after Tsogo. We really have no boundaries and we love working with Alumni. Keep in mind that all of this is done through volunteering of skills and donations from other Alumni. Supporting each other leads to a better community.
