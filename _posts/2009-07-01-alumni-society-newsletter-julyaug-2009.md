### Introduction

Dear friends the 3rd Tsogo Alumni Society Newsletter has been uploaded for your reading pleasure, and as a special treat we have also uploaded an invite to the 3rd Annual Careers Expo which will be taking place in September 2009.

[![Tsogo Alumni Society Newsletter](https://i0.wp.com/tsogoalumni.org.za/wp-content/uploads/2009/07/Tsogo-Alumni-Society-Newsletter.png?resize=208%2C294 "Tsogo Alumni Society Newsletter")](/wp-content/uploads/2009/07/Tsogo-Alumni-Society-Newsletter-Issue-No.3-July-091.pdf)

Download: [Alumni Newsletter Issue No.3 (July ’09)](/wp-content/uploads/2009/07/Tsogo-Alumni-Society-Newsletter-Issue-No.3-July-091.pdf)

### Highlights in this issue include:

1. A brief report on how the school performed in last year’s (2008) Matric results.

2. The 2nd Annual Alumni Careers Expo hosted at the school.

3. Exciting news about Tsogo’s new Mathematics Laboratory which was officially opened in June 2008.

4. An introduction you to useful alumni communication platforms which can be used to access information about the alumni, school and our activities.

5. Finally, we wrap up this issue by giving you our plans for the rest of 2009, some general information about the society and requesting your support and donations.

### Career Expo

Attached is also an invite (more so a ‘teaser’) for the 3rd Annual Careers Expo to be held in late September. The exact dates and some draft agenda will be released to alumni in the 1st or 2nd week of August . This ‘teaser’ is simply meant to make alumni and friends of Tsogo aware of the upcoming event so they can start planning and thinking about it.

Once again thank you for your interest in Tsogo Secondary School. We remain confident that our humble efforts will go a long way in contributing to the creation of future industry and society leaders from within the pool of Tsogo alumni.
