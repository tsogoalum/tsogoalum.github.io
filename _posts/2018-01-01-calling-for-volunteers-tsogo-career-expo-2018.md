For the 9th Tsogo Alumni Career and Study Expo (to be held on the 16th March 2018), we are calling for volunteers to serve on an Adhoc organising committee. Assistance is needed in general event planning, sourcing of services and event management. If you would like to assist please email executive@tsogoalumni.org.za with your name and contact details.

A great way to give back.

Responses are needed by 2 February 2018.
