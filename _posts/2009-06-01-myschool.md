My school is described as “South Africa’s biggest community support and fundraising programme, raising over R1.5 million for schools and charities every month. You can assist schools, community projects, charities and environmental protection programs through this unique fundraising activity.”

Tsogo is one of the schools registered with MySchool. If you would like to apply for a card please visit their website at [http://www.myschool.co.za](http://www.myschool.co.za/)
