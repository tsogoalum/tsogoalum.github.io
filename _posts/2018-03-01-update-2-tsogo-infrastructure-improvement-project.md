Update from Thabiso Setshedi (Alumni Society Treasurer) – *After sourcing a number of quotations, a contractor recommended by the Tsogo Class of 1983 has started refurbishing the Tsogo Secondary Toilet facilities*. Below are some pictures of the progress so far. The project has kicked off with the proceeds for the 2017 golf day and a 2nd phase of fundraising is about to begin to grow the project.

You can read about our first update, and fundraising effort [here.](/2017/12/golf-day-report-and-save-the-date-for-the-2018-tsogo-alumni-fundraising-golf-day) Our aim is to raise [R1 million](/society/tsogo-infrastructure-improvement-project) to improve the infrastructure at the school. We have raised close to R180,000.00 in 2017 and aim to raise a further R300,000.00 in 2018. The current projected cost of the toilet facility refurbishment is R160,000.00. [Donate today](/society/fundraising).

Thabiso Setshedi, Thabo Ncalo are currently overseeing the project on behalf of the alumni society.
