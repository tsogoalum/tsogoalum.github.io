[![](https://i0.wp.com/tsogoalumni.org.za/wp-content/uploads/2012/06/MRC.png?resize=74%2C95 "MRC")](https://i0.wp.com/tsogoalumni.org.za/wp-content/uploads/2012/06/MRC.png)The MRC offers a number of bursaries and training scholarships for full-time research training at universities and technikons. MRC bursaries and scholarships will be awarded to South African citizens or permanent residents only. The MRC must be acknowledged in all publications and other outputs. MRC scholarships are exempted from personal tax and the institution at which the individual is based must apply for a tax directive for additional income, where applicable.

Go to their website for more information: [link](http://www.mrc.ac.za/funding/researchopport.htm)
