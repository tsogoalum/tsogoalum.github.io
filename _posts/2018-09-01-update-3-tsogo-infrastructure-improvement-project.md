The work to upgrade the bathroom facilities at Tsogo is complete. Thank to all Alumni who supported the [Tsogo Infrastructure project in 2017](/society/tsogo-infrastructure-improvement-project). (Raising close to R200k).

Join us now in 2018 as we aim to raise more money to continue the project. See the golf day details for the [26 October 2018](/2018/09/support-us-tsogo-alumni-1st-annual-fundraising-golf-day-1).
