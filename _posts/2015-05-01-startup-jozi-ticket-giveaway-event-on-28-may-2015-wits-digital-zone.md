## Event Details

#### Trevor Wolfe: Learn how skills crowdsourcing can help you make a success of your project or startup

iAfrikan will be hosting Trevor Wolfe on 28 May 2015. He will be giving tips and talking about how to use crowdsourcing to help you with your projects whether you’re a student, freelancer, run projects in a company or a startup founder. Trevor will also speak about his experiences as Managing Director and Head of Commercialisation at Springleap and lessons learnt from pivoting and running their business.

Time: 18h30 (GMT +2)  
Venue: Wits Digital Innovation Zone (111 Smit Street)

Come learn, network and be inspired. You could also meet your future co-founder, employee, investor. Drinks and food will be served.
