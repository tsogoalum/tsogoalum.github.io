Below are the Matric Results for Tsogo Secondary school for the year 2014. The information has been gathered from multiple sources and is referenced as such. The information will be updated throughout the day as more sources become available.

Congratulations Matriculants, you  are now Alumni. Keep in touch by joining our mailing list. [Join HERE](http://bit.ly/tsogoMatric2014Contact).

*Disclaimer: We have tried to make all the information as accurate as possible given our sources, if we have made a mistake please [contact us](/about "Contact").*

Tsogo Secondary had a total of 98 students who passed out of 103. Of those who passed, 61(-12 from [2013](/2014/01/matric-results-2013 "Tsogo Secondary Matric Results 2013")) Obtained pass with Admission to Bachelor Degree , 29(-10) with Admission to Diploma and 8(0) with Admission to Higher Certificate.

Tsogo Matriculants can check their results here: <http://matric.ewn.co.za/dbe/2014/north-west/0600102264/>
