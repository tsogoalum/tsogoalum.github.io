On 6th December 2017, the Alumni Society handed over a cheque for R185,000 to the school – being the proceeds from the 1st Annual Tsogo Alumni Fundraising Golf Day. The funds will be used to fully renovate the learners’ toilets, starting in January 2018.

![Society Vice Presiden, Moshabi Pitsoane, handing over the proceeds to Mr. Malemane.](https://i0.wp.com/tsogoalumni.org.za/wp-content/uploads/2017/12/24991161_1735399853146394_7864440053634590402_n.jpg?resize=300%2C225)

Society Vice President, Moshabi Pitsoane, handing over the proceeds to Mr. Malemane.

Thank you to all the supporters, players and donors!! You can read our report here [Tsogo Alumni Society 2017 Golf Day Report](/wp-content/uploads/2017/12/Tsogo-Alumni-Society-Golf-Day-v.Final_.pdf)
