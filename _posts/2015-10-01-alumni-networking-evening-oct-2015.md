The Tsogo Alumni Society held a networking evening for Alumni at Stanlib (thanks to Mr. Thabo Ncalo) in Melrose Arch on the evening of the 29th October 2015. We are very thankful for those who were able to make it. We hope for this to be the first of many. Look out for more events in future. If you haven’t already, join our mailing list. Look at [“The Alumni Starter Pack”](/social-networking/the-alumni-starter-pack). If you would like to help host (get a venue, logistics of the day etc.) let us know by emailing us at tsogoalum@gmail.com
